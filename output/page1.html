fMD Survey

Welcome to our test survey, in this survey you will see the rendered HTML of a fMD document. A fMD document is short for a formsMarkdown document, which is an addition to Markdown done by 0yqc. In this addition you can just type questions by beginning a line with '?' followed by a still-readable syntax for defining questions. Although this was written for testing purposes, I appreciate responses to this, as it's also about further development of formsMD
